Rear Window (1954)

Rating: 8.5 out of 10

Release year: 1954

Reviews: Rear Window is a 1954 American suspense film directed by Alfred Hitchcock and starring James Stewart, Grace Kelly, and Wendell Corey. The film tells the story of a wheelchair-bound photographer who becomes obsessed with spying on his neighbors through the rear window of his apartment, eventually witnessing what he believes to be a murder. The film was widely acclaimed upon its release and is considered one of Hitchcock's greatest films. It received positive reviews for its suspenseful plot, strong performances, and Hitchcock's direction.The film was a commercial success and was nominated for several awards, including four Academy Awards.

Se7en

Rating: 8.6 out of 10

Release year: 1995

Reviews: Se7en is a 1995 crime thriller film directed by David Fincher and written by Andrew Kevin Walker. The film stars Brad Pitt, Morgan Freeman, Gwyneth Paltrow, and Kevin Spacey. The film tells the story of two detectives who are on the hunt for a serial killer who chooses his victims based on the seven deadly sins.

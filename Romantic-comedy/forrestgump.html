Forrest Gump

Rating: 8.8/10

Release year: 1994

Reviews: Forrest Gump is a heartwarming and comedic film that tells the story of a man who, despite his below average intelligence, leads an extraordinary life. The film is filled with memorable moments and is sure to make you laugh and cry.

Cast of the movie: The film stars Tom Hanks as Forrest Gump, Robin Wright as Jenny Curran, Gary Sinise as Lieutenant Dan Taylor, and Sally Field as Forrest's mother. Other notable cast members include Mykelti Williamson as Bubba Blue, Hanna Hall as Young Forrest, and Michael Conner Humphreys as Young Forrest.

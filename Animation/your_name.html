Your Name (2016)

Rating: 8.4 out of 10

Release year: 2016

Reviews: "Your Name" (Japanese title: "Kimi no Na Wa") is a 2016 Japanese animated romantic fantasy film directed by Makoto Shinkai and produced by CoMix Wave Films. The film tells the story of a high school girl in rural Japan and a high school boy in Tokyo who discover they can swap bodies. The two of them begin to communicate by leaving notes, messages, and more in each other's lives, ultimately leading to an unexpected meeting. The film was a critical and commercial success, grossing over $360 million worldwide. It received widespread acclaim for its animation, storytelling, and emotional depth. It holds a rating of 8.4 out of 10 on IMDb and 96% on Rotten Tomatoes. The film was nominated for several awards and won several awards including the 40th Japan Academy Prize for Animation of the Year. It has become one of the highest-grossing anime film of all time and is considered a modern anime classic.

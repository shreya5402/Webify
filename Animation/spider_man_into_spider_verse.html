Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse (2018)

Rating: 8.4 out of 10

Release year: 2018

Reviews: Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse is a 2018 American computer-animated superhero film directed by Bob Persichetti, Peter Ramsey, and Rodney Rothman. The film is produced by Columbia Pictures and Sony Pictures Animation and it's based on the Marvel Comics character Miles Morales / Spider-Man, a teenager of Afro-Latino descent who becomes the Spider-Man of his universe after the death of Peter Parker. The film features the voices of Shameik Moore as Miles Morales/Spider-Man, Jake Johnson as Peter B. Parker/Spider-Man, Hailee Steinfeld as Gwen Stacy/Spider-Woman, John Mulaney as Peter Porker/Spider-Ham, Nicolas Cage as Peter Parker/Spider-Man Noir, and Liev Schreiber as Wilson Fisk/Kingpin.

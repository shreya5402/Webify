Reviews: The Lord of the Rings is a film series of three epic fantasy adventure films directed by Peter Jackson, based on the novel "The Lord of the Rings" by J. R. R. Tolkien. The series consists of: The Fellowship of the Ring (2001) The Two Towers (2002) The Return of the King (2003) The series stars Elijah Wood, Ian McKellen, Viggo Mortensen, Liv Tyler, Sean Astin, Cate Blanchett, John Rhys-Davies, Billy Boyd, Dominic Monaghan, Orlando Bloom, Christopher Lee, Hugo Weaving, and Andy Serkis. The film series was a critical and commercial success and grossed over $2.9 billion worldwide. It received numerous accolades, including 17 Academy Awards, four Golden Globe Awards and four British Academy Film Awards. The Lord of the Rings is widely considered to be one of the greatest film series ever made and it has become a cultural phenomenon.

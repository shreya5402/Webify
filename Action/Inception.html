Inception

Rating: 8.8 out of 10

Release year: 2010

Reviews: Inception is a 2010 science fiction action film directed, written, produced and co-edited by Christopher Nolan, and starring Leonardo DiCaprio, Ken Watanabe, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Marion Cotillard, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, Dileep Rao, Cillian Murphy, Tom Berenger, and Michael Caine. The film tells the story of a professional thief who is given the task of planting an idea in someone's mind through a process known as "inception". The film was a commercial and critical success, grossing over $829 million worldwide. It received widespread acclaim for its complex and original story, strong performances, and Nolan's direction.

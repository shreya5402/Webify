The Matrix

Rating: 8.7 out of 10

Release year: 1999

Reviews: The Matrix is a 1999 science fiction action film directed by the Wachowski Brothers and starring Keanu Reeves, Laurence Fishburne, Carrie-Anne Moss, Hugo Weaving, and Joe Pantoliano. The film tells the story of a hacker who is drawn into a rebellion against a dystopian future in which humanity is trapped inside a simulated reality called the Matrix. The film was a critical and commercial success, grossing over $460 million worldwide. It received positive reviews for its action sequences, special effects, and philosophical themes, and it has a rating of 8.7 out of 10 on IMDb and 87% on Rotten Tomatoes. The Matrix has since become a cultural phenomenon and has had a significant impact on the science fiction and action genre. It also won four Academy Awards.

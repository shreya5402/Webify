The Dark Knight

Rating: 9.0 out of 10

Release year: 2008

Reviews: The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan and starring Christian Bale as Batman, Heath Ledger as the Joker, Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent/Two-Face, Gary Oldman as Commissioner Gordon, and Maggie Gyllenhaal as Rachel Dawes. The film is the second installment in Nolan's The Dark Knight Trilogy and it is a sequel to Batman Begins (2005). The film was a critical and commercial success and grossed over $1 billion worldwide. It received widespread acclaim for its performances, particularly Ledger's portrayal of the Joker, and for its dark, complex and realistic take on the Batman character.
